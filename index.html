Oreo's History

Over 100 years ago in 1912, Nabisco introduced the Oreo to the cookie market. Originally meant to be a more elegant alternative to a cookie already in the market called the Hydrox. Although the original 2 chocolate disks seperated by a cream filling architure has stayed true, over the years Oreo has introduced many different flavors.
